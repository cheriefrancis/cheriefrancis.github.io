Projects



Nerdy Critters

These are sancasted, animal shaped glasses and jewellery holders that were made as a final project for the class ME103: Product Realization: Design and Making. The bear was made from bronze and the cat from aluminium.

Cherieboutin

This is a high heeled shoe which was 3d printed with PLA using an ender-3. The heel is of an uncinventional shape since it was designed using the shape optimisation software in Fusion 360.





Candy Dispenser

This candy dispenser was mainly laser cut and possesses a 3d printed funnel and candy holder component. It also consists of a spring which returns the holder to its original position to dispense the next piece of candy.





You Spin My Hand Right Round: Learning about Human Perception of Mechanical Systems

This haptic wheel was my 2021 summer research project in which I designed a 3D Printed haptic wheel and programmed it to turn to user-specified angles using Arduino under supervision of a graduate research mentor. The file below is a poster that gives a background to haptics, how the wheel works as well as future work and possible applications.
